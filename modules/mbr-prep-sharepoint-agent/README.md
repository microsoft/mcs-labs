# Monthly Business Review Agent

This module introduces the idea of using Copilot Studio to support monthly business review preparation with content stored in SharePoint. It focuses on how agents can help summarize information, surface relevant files, and reduce the manual effort involved in preparing for recurring business review meetings.

The module also highlights the importance of permissions, document organization, and prompt design when working with internal business content. The companion lab demonstrates how these concepts come together in a SharePoint-grounded scenario built for practical review preparation.

## Key Topics

- SharePoint-grounded agent scenarios
- Preparing for recurring business reviews
- Summarization and content retrieval
- Permissions and internal knowledge access
