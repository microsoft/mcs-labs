# Measure Success & Analytics

This module provides the conceptual foundation for measuring whether an agent is actually delivering value. It introduces the analytics, metrics, and success criteria that teams should use to evaluate adoption, experience quality, and business impact over time.

The goal is to help learners move beyond anecdotal feedback and toward evidence-based improvement. The companion lab reinforces these ideas by showing how to interpret analytics and use them to guide the next round of optimization.

## Key Topics

- Defining meaningful success metrics
- Interpreting analytics and trends
- Connecting insights to improvements
- Evaluating adoption and business impact
