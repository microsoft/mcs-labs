# Bring Your Own Model (BYOM)

This module introduces bring your own model patterns for teams that want to connect custom AI models to Copilot Studio experiences. It explains the reasons an organization might choose a specialized model, such as domain specificity, existing investments, or unique performance requirements.

Learners also examine the architectural and operational considerations involved in integrating external models, including reliability, governance, and evaluation. The companion lab gives participants a practical way to explore how custom model integration can extend standard agent capabilities.

## Key Topics

- Why use a custom model
- Integrating external AI models
- Operational and governance considerations
- Extending Copilot Studio with BYOM patterns
