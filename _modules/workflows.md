> **Workflows are the new autonomous agents.** This module introduces the
> Workflows capability in Copilot Studio as the next step beyond the classic
> autonomous-agent trigger model:
>
> - **Autonomous by design** — an agent that runs on its own rather than waiting for a user turn.
> - **Inline agent connections** — compose and call agents directly inside a workflow.
> - **Scheduled & event-driven triggers** — kick work off on a timer or in response to an event, not just a chat message.
